How to Play
You are Olive. Forest is the AI opponent.
🧠 = neural model active   🤖 = heuristic fallback.
Click a unit to select it.
■ Blue = free reposition (unit stays ready).
■ Gold = action (capture / merge / clear).
Units move freely within territory.
Only captures & merges consume the move.
Merge units by strength: levels add up (max 4).
P = Peasant, S = Spearman
K = Knight, B = Baron
🏠 = Hut (str 1)   🏰 = Tower (str 2)
Strength must be greater to capture.
Pay upkeep or units die (→ gravestones).
■ Green = place unit here.
■ Yellow-green = clears the tree.
■ Teal = merge with existing unit.
■ Orange = parachute to undefended hex.
Buy any unit directly: Peasant 5g, Spearman 10g, Knight 15g, Baron 20g.
